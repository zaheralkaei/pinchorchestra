Quarter-tone lowering (−50 cents)
These buttons lower the pitch by a quarter tone (−50 cents). It affects both hands, both octaves and the drone.
How to play:
• Put your index fingertip on a circle and pinch thumb+index to hold a note.
• You can play two notes at once (two hands).
• Higher octave is on the right. Keyboard is always starting at C.
• Up/Down (Y) controls brightness (higher = brighter).

Audio: Press Start Audio once (browser policy). It becomes Stop Audio.
